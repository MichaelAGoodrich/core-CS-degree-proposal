# CS 340: Software Engineering

## Justification

CS 340 is included in the junior core curriculum as currently taught with no proposed changes. This course is already a required component of the general computer science degree and remains essential for students entering the field.

### Rationale for Inclusion

- **Industry Preparation**: Software engineering principles directly address professional practices students will encounter in industry
- **System Design and Teamwork**: The course develops critical skills in project management, design patterns, and collaborative development
- **Practical Integration**: Software engineering concepts integrate theoretical knowledge from other core courses into real-world practices
- **Program Strength**: The existing course effectively serves student learning and requires no modifications

We maintain this course as-is to honor the principle of evolutionary rather than revolutionary change, recognizing that the course is already meeting its intended learning outcomes effectively.
