# CS 270 and CS 370: Scope, Goals, and Comparative Coverage

## Course Descriptions

### CS 270 — Introduction to Machine Learning

**Course Goal:**  
Understand the fundamental models of machine learning, such as neural networks, decision trees, data mining, clustering, Bayesian learning, ensembles, reinforcement learning, and deep learning. Work with data and machine learning tools in real-world applications.

Source: https://catalog.byu.edu/courses/dBwXcrBxxGfbtBqMvDyJ

---

### CS 370 — Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

CS 370 focuses on building strong mental models of AI paradigms, understanding when and why they apply, comparing their strengths and limitations, and reasoning about their ethical and societal implications. Goals, learning outcomes, and teaching methodology emphasize paradigm-level understanding, experimentation, and ethical reflection.
