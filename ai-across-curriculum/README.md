# AI Across the Curriculum

AI needs to be integrated across many classes in the curriculum, not isolated to a single course. This directory is included to differentiate what belongs in the required AI class (CS 370) versus AI-related concepts that should appear elsewhere in the curriculum. These documents are not intended to replace the broader conversation currently taking place in the CS department, but rather to provide information that the committee considered the broader context when choosing to propose a new required AI course.

- **[AI_and_CS_Jobs_Selected_Perspectives.md](AI_and_CS_Jobs_Selected_Perspectives.md)** — Selected industry perspectives on AI skills expected of CS graduates
