## Top Three Strengths

1. Math foundations (21 support, 1 do not support)
2. Experiential learning requirement (21 support, 1 do not support)
3. Vision statement (20 support, 2 do not support)

For details, see [top_8_strengths.md](top_8_strengths.md) and [cs_degree_survey_summary.md](cs_degree_survey_summary.md).

## Top Three Weaknesses (Prioritized Concerns)

1. AI integration in the curriculum
2. Breadth/depth structure design
3. Mentoring capacity and quality control in experiential learning

For details, see [top_6_prioritized_concerns.md](top_6_prioritized_concerns.md), [top_6_prioritized_concerns_proposed_responses.md](top_6_prioritized_concerns_proposed_responses.md), and [more_granular_summary.md](more_granular_summary.md).
