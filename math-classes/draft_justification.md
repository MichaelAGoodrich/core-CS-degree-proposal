## Rationale

The math and analytical reasoning components follow three guiding principles:

1. **Shared mathematical foundation:** use established math classes that all students complete before entering the junior core.
2. **Alignment with ACM guidelines:** ensure the math sequence aligns with ACM curriculum recommendations for a modern CS degree.
3. **Analytical reasoning focus:** emphasize statistical and probabilistic reasoning to support data-driven thinking across the curriculum.
